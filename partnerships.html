Why Partner With Us

Access the Hopkins Community

Johns Hopkins University is one of the world's leading research institutions. Partnering with us gives your organization direct access to exceptional students and faculty working at the intersection of food, science, and sustainability.

From bioengineers to public health researchers, our members represent the diverse expertise needed to advance the alternative protein sector.

  • Recruit top students in biology, bioengineering, public health, and business
  • Collaborate on cutting-edge alternative protein research
  • Build brand awareness with a passionate, educated audience
  • Contribute to the next generation of food system leaders
  • Connect with a GFI-affiliated campus network
  • Engage with student innovators and entrepreneurs
  • Shape curriculum and hands-on learning experiences
